Green Valley Op Plan

Prior to Operation

  • Gather ground equipment - Matt/Mark
    • Grass Tie downs
    • Ratchet straps
    • Radios, chargers
    • Warning signs for road
    • Flight log, Tow cards
  • Pickup cart trailer - Matt/Mark (Charlie?)

Launch from AWO (July 5 only)

  • Approx. 9:00 am Arrive at AWO - Matt, Mark, CFI, Towpilot
  • Safety briefing
  • Prep/stage glider(s), towplane
  • (GV FM and ground crew heads to Green Valley )
    • Final check of runway, conditions
    • Communicate wind/conditions back to AWO
    • Monitor 122.8
    • Monitor mid-field access road for traffic
  • Launch ferry tow
  • Load golf cart on trailer and tow to GV
  • Land and stage glider

Saturday Operations

  • Remaining Non-towing staff arrive
  • Glider, tow plane land and stage
  • Safety briefing
    • Discuss conditions, determine launch and landing directions
    • Rope breaks and other emergencies
    • Ground ops, access road, etc.
  • Rotate flights as usual
  • Wrap-up / debrief
  • Put away gear in shed
  • Collect any garbage
  • Flight log, tow cards collected
  • Tie-down glider (grass stakes)
  • Park golf cart in T-hangar

Notes

Assembly area / power / equipment / bathroom facilities: For now, we won't have the RV this time. I'll see about access to Kyle's hangar, but it's not a given yet.

Ground crew: In addition to glider staging, one crew member will need to be stationed at the access road to address any vehicle traffic (local neighbors).

Glider staging: Two main options (land north, take off south or land/restage/take off upwind). Will be weather dependent and determined ultimately by CFIs on the field.

Towplane traffic / noise abatement: To avoid noise issues as much as possible, towplane traffic will generally favor the west side of the airport and tow gliders to the north along the river.
